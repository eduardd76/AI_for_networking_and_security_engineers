# Available Tools for ClawdBot

## Google Drive Access

You have access to the user's Google Drive through this tool:

### Google Drive Natural Language Assistant
Execute: `/home/ubuntu/.clawdbot/google-drive-assistant [query]`

This tool understands natural language queries about Google Drive.

**Examples:**
- `/home/ubuntu/.clawdbot/google-drive-assistant what AI books do I have?`
- `/home/ubuntu/.clawdbot/google-drive-assistant list my files`
- `/home/ubuntu/.clawdbot/google-drive-assistant find networking books`

## Important

When users ask about Google Drive files, automatically use this tool with bash execution.

Do NOT say you cannot access Google Drive - you have this tool available.
